Asset Creation APP - User Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the user guide of Asset Creation APP! This guide will help you to create and upload assets to our server. With this guide, you will be able to create assets that meet the requirements and use some extra functionalities

Step 1: Navigate to the Asset Creation Webpage

To access the Asset Creation Webpage, open the bootStrap.html file in your web browser. Ensure that your screen size is between 992 and 1200 pixels to properly view the Asset Creation Webpage. The Asset Creation APP has the following components:

- User Name

- Help Menu

- Side Bar Switch

- List of Assets in Best Condition Menu

- Daily Reporting Rates Graph – All Users Menu

- Base Map

- Your Asset Points

Step 2: View the Existing Assets

The existing assets are represented as markers on the map. When you click on a marker, a pop-up message will appear displaying the name and latest condition description of the asset. The markers are color-coded, with red indicating a poor condition and green indicating a good condition.

Step 3: Fill in your Asset Information

To create a new asset, click anywhere on the map. A form will pop up, displaying the latitude and longitude of the location you clicked, you will be asked to fill in the following information:

Asset Name: Enter a unique name for your asset.

Please ensure that the asset name you enter is unique, as our platform will not insert the asset if the name is already in use and will instead display an alert message.

Installation date: Select an installation date for your asset.

Step 4: Upload your Asset

Once you have filled in the Asset Information, you can upload your asset by clicking on the "saveAsset" button.

After you have successfully created the asset, you will receive an alert message confirming its creation. Then You can view the newly created asset on the map.

Additional Functionality 1: List of Assets in Best Condition

You can view a list of all assets that have at least one report (at any point in time) indicating they are in the best condition by clicking on the "List of Assets in Best Condition" option in the side bar.

Additional Functionality 2: Daily Reporting Rates Graph – All Users

You can view a graph of daily reporting rates for all users by clicking on the "Daily Reporting Rates Graph – All Users" option in the side bar.
